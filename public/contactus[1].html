Frequently Asked Questions

What are the visiting hours?

Visiting hours are from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM daily. Specific departments may have different visiting hours, so please confirm with the hospital reception.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked through our website or by calling our 24/7 helpline at 040 6165 6565.

What should I bring to my appointment?

Please bring any previous medical records, identification, and any relevant test reports. For insurance patients, bring the necessary documentation.

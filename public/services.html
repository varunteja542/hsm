Services

Patient Registration and Admission

This service manages the registration of patients, either for outpatient visits or inpatient admissions. It ensures that patient data is accurately collected and stored for future reference, streamlining the hospital's admission processes.


Appointment Scheduling

This service allows patients to schedule, modify, or cancel appointments online. It manages the availability of doctors and healthcare professionals, reducing scheduling conflicts and improving time management.


Billing and Invoicing

This service handles the creation of patient invoices, insurance claims, and payment processing. It integrates with various insurance providers to ensure smooth billing processes and handles both outpatient and inpatient charges.


Medical Consultation Services

It facilitates consultations between patients and doctors either in-person or via telemedicine. It includes scheduling appointments, managing patient queues, and ensuring patients receive appropriate medical consultations with specialists.


Emergency Care Services

Emergency care management ensures patients receive urgent medical attention in critical situations. The system helps coordinate emergency resources, ambulance services, and immediate medical interventions.
